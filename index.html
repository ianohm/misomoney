🇻🇳 Trả thêm ngay tháng đầu tiên
Start extra payments in month 1 — not month 3
Every month you wait costs more in interest. The math is unforgiving.
🇻🇳 Avalanche method = tiết kiệm nhất
Pay highest interest rate debt first
Vietnamese families often juggle multiple cards. Attack highest APR first — always.
🇻🇳 Tax refund → debt, không phải shopping
Use your tax refund to kill debt
Average family refund is $2,400+. One lump sum crushes months of interest.
🇻🇳 Đừng chỉ trả minimum payment
Minimum payment is a trap
On $10k at 20% APR, minimums take 28 years. Extra $100/mo = 7 years.

🇻🇳 Nhiều gia đình Việt bỏ qua cái này
Vietnamese families often skip the emergency fund
Cultural tendency to rely on family in emergencies. But building your own fund protects your relationships AND your finances.
🇻🇳 Để tiền ở đâu?
Use a High Yield Savings Account (HYSA)
Marcus, Ally, or SoFi — currently 4-5% APY. Your emergency fund should be earning money too.
